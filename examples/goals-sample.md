## Goal 1: Improve Platform Reliability
**Success Measures**:
- Tier-1 service availability ≥ 99.9%
- Reduce mean-time-to-detect by 40% vs. last year
- Zero repeat incidents from known root causes

## Goal 2: Accelerate Delivery
**Success Measures**:
- Decouple the top 3 monolith modules into independent deploys
- Cut average lead-time-to-production by 30%
- 50% of services on automated, zero-downtime deploys

## Goal 3: Raise Engineering Quality
**Success Measures**:
- 80% of PRs covered by automated tests
- Onboarding-to-first-PR under 2 days
- Architecture review on every initiative over 3 engineers

## Goal 4: Grow the Team
**Success Measures**:
- 2 engineers mentored with active growth plans
- Run a monthly engineering learning session
- One external talk or written piece published
